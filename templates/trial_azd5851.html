AZD5851 Clinical Trial

A Phase 1/2 Study of AZD5851 in Patients with Relapsed/Refractory B-cell Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia

NCT06084884 Recruiting Target: 120 participants
Bispecific T-cell Engager

Targeting CD19 and CD3

Study Overview

Brief Summary

This is a first-in-human, Phase 1/2, open-label, dose-escalation and dose-expansion study of AZD5851 in patients with relapsed or refractory B-cell acute lymphoblastic leukemia (B-ALL).

Detailed Description

AZD5851 is a novel bispecific T-cell engager (BiTE) that targets CD19 and CD3. This study will evaluate the safety, tolerability, pharmacokinetics, pharmacodynamics, and preliminary efficacy of AZD5851 in adult patients with relapsed/refractory B-ALL.

Key Details
  • Condition: B-cell Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia
  • Intervention: AZD5851 (Bispecific T-cell Engager)
  • Age Range: 18 Years and older
  • Gender: All
  • Sponsor: AstraZeneca
