Study Overview

Brief Summary

LOCUS is a first-in-human, Phase 1/2, open-label study evaluating AZD0486, an allogeneic CRISPR-edited CAR-T cell therapy, in patients with relapsed or refractory large B-cell lymphoma.

Detailed Description

AZD0486 is an investigational allogeneic CAR-T cell therapy using CRISPR gene editing technology to target CD19+ B-cell malignancies. The study will assess safety, tolerability, and efficacy of this novel cell therapy approach.

Key Details
